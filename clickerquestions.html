Clicker Questions

To assess student understanding of learning outcomes, we wrote clicker questions for each of our 27 topics. Clickers, or personal response systems, can be used during class to quickly assess student learning as it occurs, providing immediate feedback to both students and instructors. The complete database consists of 450 questions (need to update this when complete) across the 27 topics and is currently available in PowerPoint format using Turning Point 5.0 software.

Examples

Topic 06: Normal Distribution

Outcome C: Explain how standardizing affects the shape center, and variability of the distribution of a quantitative variable.
Outcome D: Determine which quantitative variables could be modeled using the normal distribution by interpreting graphical representations of the variable.

Topic 19: Confidence Interval for the Population Mean

Outcome B: Interpret the confidence interval for the population mean in context.
Outcome D: Verify the conditions for conducting inference for a population mean are met.
